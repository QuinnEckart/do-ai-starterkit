Add Documents

To add documents to your Knowledge Base, use the DigitalOcean GenAI Console:

  1. Click the button below to open your Knowledge Base
  2. Click "Add Data Source"
  3. Upload your files (.txt, .pdf, .md, etc.)
  4. Wait for processing to complete
  5. Come back here and start asking questions!

How It Works

When you ask a question with "Search knowledge base" enabled:

  1. Your question is sent to the DO Knowledge Base
  2. Relevant documents are found using semantic search
  3. The AI uses those documents to answer your question
  4. Sources are shown below each response
